04 Returns & refunds

Not right?
No problem.

Returns are free from France. International returns are deducted at the outbound shipping rate. Every refund processed within 7 business days.

14
Days for a full refund
Return within 14 days of receipt in original condition — unworn, undamaged — for a complete refund to your original payment method.
30
Days for store credit
Within 30 days, choose store credit instead. A unique voucher code is emailed within 7 business days, valid on any future order.
How to return — 4 steps
1
Visit the return portal
Go online to the Payote return portal. Enter your order number and delivery postcode to log in.
2
Select & choose
Pick the items to return and your reason. Then decide: full refund or store credit voucher.
3
Pack & send
Pack your items as received. Print the Colissimo return label and drop off at the nearest relay point.
4
Refund issued
Once received, your refund or voucher is processed within 7 business days. Email notification at each step.
